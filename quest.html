The Equinox OS: Master Guide 📖

1. The Discipline-Based Wealth System

Equinox is built on the philosophy that consistency is currency. Your wallet is not just a counter, but a real-time reflection of your discipline.

  • Creditable Rewards: If enabled, checking a task instantly adds the "Reward" amount to your wallet. Use this for productive habits.
  • Taxable Penalties: If enabled, failing to check a task by midnight triggers a "Penalty" deduction. This uses loss-aversion to keep you focused.
  • Required Discipline Only: You can set a task to be Taxable but NOT Creditable. This means you earn nothing for doing it, but you lose money for skipping it.

2. The Chronos Engine (Reminders)

Located in Settings, this engine tracks the time to ensure you never miss a ritual:

  • Sleep Target: Set your bedtime. Every morning, the system asks if you were on time. A "Yes" grants a ₹50 bonus.
  • Journal Alert: Set a time for your nightly reflection. A popup will trigger at that exact minute.
  • Task Reminders: Add custom alerts for specific times of day (e.g. 14:00 - Start Lab Session).

3. Modular Systems

You can turn modules on or off in the Module Manager to keep your UI clean:

  • Hydration: Operates on a Sliding Tax. You must drink 4 bottles to avoid penalties. 0 bottles = ₹40 tax; 4 bottles = ₹0 tax.
  • Project Tracker: The OS scans for the keyword "Project" in your tasks. If found, it sums the hours into the "Project Work" analytic in your archives.
  • Debt Mode: If on, you can spend into the negative. The wallet turns Rose, signaling Restoration Mode.

4. Multi-Quest Weekly Planner

Assign specific tasks to specific days. Use + ADD QUEST in the Weekly Plan tab. These Quests will automatically appear in your Daily Journal at midnight on their assigned day, each with its own checkbox.

5. Persistence & Design

  • Thought Vault: Every reflection you write is dated and moved here during the midnight reset.
  • Snapshot Mode: The camera icon hides all buttons for aesthetic logging. A floating camera remains to exit the mode.
  • Theming: Change any color or select from 13+ presets in the Design Studio.
