Equinox Operative Manual 📖

1. Core Architecture

The Equinox is a Personal OS designed to bridge the gap between productivity and self-reflection. It operates on two distinct logical frameworks:

  • Reward-Based Economy: Your discipline has a literal value. Completing tasks earns "Currency" (₹), while failing taxable habits deducts it. This turns your day into a balanced budget.
  • Simple Journal: Removes all fiscal elements (Wallet/Finance) for those who prefer a minimalist, purely task-focused experience.

2. The Task Ecosystem

There are three ways tasks enter your dashboard:

  • The Daily Goals (Settings): These are your recurring habits (e.g., "Drink Water", "Workout"). They reset every morning automatically.
  • Weekly Plan: Specific quests scheduled for certain days. Unlike habits, these are usually one-off goals for that specific week.
  • Manual Injection: Use the Sync Habits or Sync Weekly buttons on the dashboard to pull tasks into your day if you missed the midnight transition.

3. Syllabus Intelligence 🧠

A specialized module for students or researchers. You define a Subject Name and the Total Chapters in Settings. The OS uses Pattern Recognition: if you complete a task that contains the subject's name (e.g., "Study Physics 101" for a Physics subject), the system automatically increments your progress bar.

4. The Remainders

Time management is handled via the in Settings:

  • Sleep Target: If you wake up and confirm you met your sleep target, you earn a flat bonus (₹50 in reward mode).
  • Journal Alert: A system-wide modal that reminds you to log your thoughts at your preferred reflection time.
  • Custom Reminders: Set specific times for "Daily Quests" to trigger a popup alert.

5. Privacy & Presence

Snapshot Mode (📸): Click the camera icon or button to hide all interactive elements (inputs, buttons, nav). This is designed for capturing a beautiful "clean view" of your progress or for focusing on reading without distraction. Click again to return.

Eclipse View (✨): Instantly toggles between Light and Dark mode. Dark mode is optimized for nighttime reflection.

6. Data Sovereignty

The Equinox saves data locally to your browser's LocalStorage. It never leaves your device. We recommend using the 💾 Export button in Settings once a week to keep a physical backup of your life data.
